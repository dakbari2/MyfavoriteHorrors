Best Horror Movies

Ranking of my top 3 favorite horror movies

Candyman

#1 in my favorite horror movies

The new Candyman that came out in 2021 is honestly the best because it's a mix of horror, art, and suspense. I love the sense of madness that the characters feel when they say Candyman's name and invoke him. The characters can't tell if he is real or if he is just in their heads.

Us

#2 in my favorite horror movies

This movie is an intriguing Horror that has many twists with originality in concept because you see characters that talk to each other and look exactly the same. You have to follow the movie to understand the hidden meaning and plot. The visuals and location settings are phenomenal.

Ma

#3 in my favorite horror movies

Ma is a psychological horror and the audience needs to watch it from Ma's point of view. The movie portays her own personal experiences that she lived during High School. She is trying to experience those moments again through a group of young adults but this time she wants to change the traumatic narrative.
